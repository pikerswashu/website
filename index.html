Hi! We're the Pikers.

We're an all-male a cappella group, and the oldest a cappella group at WashU, founded in 1985. We all share a common love for music, despite coming from many backgrounds - some of us having extensive musical experience, and others little to none. In all cases, we come together to sing. Whether it's a sorority gig or our annual campus concert, Jammin' Toast, we strive to bring the most energetic and exciting energy possible to our performances. And the most ridiculous. We're very good at that.
